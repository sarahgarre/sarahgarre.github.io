Welkom in de wereld van water, landbouw en klimaat!

If you want to know more about the role of water used for Flemish agriculture in the context of climate change, you've come to the right place. With this interactive poster I present the importance of water in agriculture for today and tomorrow. How can Flemish agriculture adapt to the consequences of a changing climate on water availability and quality?
What are the challenges and especially, what are possible solutions?
TO THE INTERACTIVE POSTER
How does it work?
By moving your mouse over the drawing you get to see content that is linked to the questions on the drawing. For some items you can click through to a page with more information or to the page of an internal/external project around that theme. For each item you will also find a number of contacts within ILVO who have expertise around this theme. Do not hesitate to contact them for more information.
This poster is part of the Centre of Expertise for Agriculture and Climate (ELK) and was created by Sarah Garré in collaboration with many ILVO colleagues To the ELK website
